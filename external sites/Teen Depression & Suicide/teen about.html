About Depression and Where to Start

With 14% of teenagers having a depressive episode within the last 12 months and 4 out of 5 teens giving clear warnings of depression before a suicide attempt, the signs of dperession should be clear..shouldn't they?

Most importantly are to know the symptoms of depression so you can know if you suffer from it.
The most prominent negative feelings are...

  • Negative Feelings
  • Fatigue
  • Short Temper/Irribality
  • Poor Sleep
  • Issues Concentrating
  • Anxiety
  • General Stress
  • Changes in Weight
  • Sexual Changes
  • Drug Addiction
  • Suicidal Thoughts

If you think you suffer from any of these symptoms, then there are many ways to find help.
